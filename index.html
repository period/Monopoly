Monopoly

A text adventure which stretches the definition of text adventure. Multiplayer as standard.

Hints & Tricks

  • Click on a property on the board for additional information about that property and access to purchase etc.
  • Full set of properties is required to add houses, etc.
  • Click 'Next Round' once you've finished with your turn in order to speed the game up.
